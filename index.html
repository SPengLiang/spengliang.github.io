Liang Peng (彭亮)

I am a full-time algorithm engineer at FABU, and I was an intern at FABU in 2019-2023. I received Ph.D.degree in State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University in 2023, supervised by Prof. Deng Cai and Prof. Xiaofei He. I got my bachelor's degree from measurement and control technology and instruments at Hefei University of Technology in 2018.

I am interested in generative models (text2img, text2video, video2video) and 3D scene understanding.
